# Cigga Go Rulebook

## Overview

Cigga Go is a drafting party game themed around Chinese cigarette culture. Collect cigarette pack cards, build brand and region combos, dodge counterfeits, and play items to mess with your friends. After 3 rounds, whoever has the most points wins.

> 3-7 players · 10-25 minutes · Ages 14+
>
> Expansion supports 8 players

### 2. Build the Deck

1. Shuffle all 134 Cigarette Pack cards, 5 Counterfeit cards, and 8 Item cards together

3. Shuffle this deck well — this is your game deck

## How to Play

A game is exactly **3 rounds**. Each round follows the same steps. After all 3 rounds, add up your round scores — highest total wins.

### Passing Direction

| Round | Direction |
|:-----:|:---------:|
| 1 | Left |
| 2 | Right |
| 3 | Left |

### Round Steps

#### 1. Deal

Deal cards from the deck to each player (see table above). Look at your hand — keep it secret.

> Blue-backed cards are Items. You'll spot them right away.

#### 2. Pick

Everyone **simultaneously** picks **1 card** from their hand and places it **face-down** in front of them.

#### 3. Pass

Hand your remaining cards to the next player in the current passing direction.

#### 4. Reveal

Everyone **simultaneously** flips their picked card face-up for all to see.

#### 5. Repeat

Pick 1 from your new hand, pass, reveal... keep going until everyone has only **2 cards** left in hand.

#### 6. Final Choice

From your last 2 cards:
- **Keep 1** (place face-down with your collection)
- **Discard 1 face-up** in the center of the table for all to see

#### 7. Grab Round

1. Flip the **top card** of the remaining deck face-up
2. Everyone **slaps for it** — first to touch it takes it
3. That card **counts toward this round's score**

> **Careful:** If it's a Counterfeit, the grabber eats -5 points. Sometimes the smartest move is keeping your hands to yourself.

#### 8. Score

Everyone reveals their full collection and calculates their round score (see Scoring below). Record it on the score pad.

#### 9. Clear

Collect all cards, shuffle them back into the deck, and start the next round.

## Scoring

### Scoring Order

```
1. Add up base points from all your Cigarette Pack cards
2. Add all combo bonuses
3. Apply item card effects
4. If Grand Slam triggered → multiply everything above by x2
5. Subtract -5 for each Counterfeit card
6. That's your round score
```

### Base Points

| Rarity | Points |
|:------:|:------:|
| ★★ | 2 |
| ★★★ | 3 |
| ★★★★ | 5 |
| ★★★★★ | 7 |

### Combo Table

| Combo | Condition | Bonus |
|-------|-----------|:-----:|
| **Pair** | Same brand x2 | +5 |
| **Three-of-a-Kind** | Same brand x3 | +12 |
| **Hometown** | Same origin x2 | +3 |
| **Old Friends** | Same origin x3 | +8 |
| **Format Clash** | All 3 formats present (Regular + Slim + Medium) | +4 |
| **Mixed Bag** | 6 cards, all different brands AND all different origins | +8 |
| **Grand Slam** | Same brand x3, each a different format | Total x2 |

**Stacking:** All additive combos stack freely — the same cards can trigger multiple combos at once. Grand Slam's x2 multiplier applies last, after all additions.

### Scoring Example

**Example 1: Playing It Safe**

You kept 7 cards: Furong Wang (Hunan) x2, Yunyan (Yunnan) x1, Yuxi (Yunnan) x1, Liqun (Zhejiang) x1, Huanghelou (Hubei) x1, Nanjing (Jiangsu) x1 — all ★★★

- Base points: 3 x 7 = 21
- Pair (Furong Wang x2): +5
- Hometown (Yunnan x2): +3
- **Round score: 21 + 5 + 3 = 29**

**Example 2: Going for Broke**

You kept 7 cards: Furong Wang (Hunan/★★) x3 (one Regular, one Slim, one Medium), Baisha (Hunan/★★) x1, Huangshan (Anhui/★★★) x1, two random ★★★ cards. Plus a Zippo Lighter.

- Base points: 2x3 + 2 + 3 + 3x2 = 16
- Three-of-a-Kind (Furong Wang x3): +12
- Old Friends (Hunan x4, need 3): +8
- Format Clash (all 3 formats): +4
- Zippo Lighter (highest combo +5): +5
- Subtotal: 16 + 12 + 8 + 4 + 5 = 45
- Grand Slam (Furong Wang x3, each a different format): 45 x 2 = **90 points!**

## Counterfeit Cards

There are **5 Counterfeit cards** shuffled into the deck.

- **Appearance:** Same card back as regular packs. The front looks like a premium card — famous brand, high rarity — but it's marked with a small "fake" symbol
- **Effect:** Each Counterfeit costs **-5 points**, subtracted after all combos and Grand Slam
- **The fun part:** You'll pick one up thinking you struck gold, then notice the mark and groan. You can take the hit or pass the card along and let someone else fall for it. The post-scoring blame game of "who gave me that fake?" is one of the best moments in the game

---

## Item Cards

Item cards have **blue backs** — you'll see them the moment cards are dealt. Picking an item means one fewer Pack card in your collection, so make it count.

**Max 1 item card per player per round.**

| Item | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Zippo Lighter** | Your highest combo this round gets +5 |
| **Moutai** | Pick a player — you both get +4 |
| **Mahjong Table** | Your Old Friends combo becomes +13 this round |
| **Macau Casino** | Flip the coin: heads +10, tails -5 |
| **Counterfeit Certificate** | Cancel the -5 penalty from 1 Counterfeit you hold |
| **Black Market Pass** | Retrieve 1 card from this round's discard pile |
| **Smuggling Channel** | Swap 1 of your collected cards with 1 from any other player |
| **Anti-Smoking Fine** | Target a player — their highest combo bonus is halved (round down) |

## Advanced Rule: Inspection

> We recommend skipping this for your first game. Add it once everyone knows the basics.

**How it works:**
- Each player gets **1 Inspection** per round
- During the drafting phase, point at **any player's most recently picked face-down card** and call out "Inspection!"
- That player must flip the card for everyone to see
  - **It's fake** — the Counterfeit goes back to whoever passed it. Nice catch!
  - **It's real** — you blew it. You lose **3 points** this round

**Reading people:** Watch how others pick their cards. Grabbing one instantly? Hesitating? Trying not to smile? Those are all tells.

## Tips for New Players

1. **Pairs first.** A Pair (+5) is easy to pull off. Three-of-a-Kind (+12) takes commitment — don't force it.
2. **Watch the region colors.** Each card has a colored region badge. Same color means same origin, which means potential combos.
3. **Check for fakes.** When you get an amazing-looking card, look for the fake mark before you celebrate.
4. **Mixed Bag is your fallback.** If someone cuts off the combo you were building, pivot to 6 cards with all different brands and origins for a solid +8.
5. **Items cost a card slot.** Every item you draft is one fewer cigarette pack. Make sure the trade-off is worth it.
6. **Grand Slam is a miracle, not a strategy.** Same brand x3 with each in a different format? If it lands, incredible. If you chase it, you'll probably end up with nothing.
7. **Round 2 flips the pass direction.** The player you're feeding cards to changes — rethink your game plan.
8. **Think before you slap.** In the Grab Round, that card might be a Counterfeit. Sometimes holding back is the winning move.
9. **Format Clash is free points.** One Regular, one Slim, one Medium — that's +4 for just having variety.
